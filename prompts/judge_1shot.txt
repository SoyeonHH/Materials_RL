## Synthesis Procedure

### Synthesis of Multi-Shelled ZnO Spheres
1. Dissolve 130 g of sucrose in 250 mL deionized water, stir for 30 min at room temperature.
2. Transfer to a 500 mL Teflon-lined stainless steel autoclave and heat at 200°C for 125 min.
3. Cool to room temperature, wash with deionized water and ethanol, then dry at 60°C for 12 h to obtain carbon microsphere templates.
4. Dissolve 45 g zinc nitrate hexahydrate in 30 mL mixed solution (10 mL ethanol and 20 mL deionized water).
5. Disperse 1 g carbon microsphere templates in the solution, ultrasonicate for 15 min, then stir at 40°C for 12 h.
6. Filter, wash, dry at 80°C for 12 h, then calcine: 
   - Heat to 400°C at 1°C/min, maintain for 30 min.
   - Further heat to 500°C, maintain for 60 min, cool naturally.

### Synthesis of CQDs
1. Dissolve 11.7 g sodium citrate and 0.5 g ammonium dihydrogen phosphate in 40 mL deionized water.
2. Stir for 15 min at room temperature to form a solution.
3. Transfer to a 100 mL Teflon-lined stainless steel autoclave, maintain at 160°C for 6 h.
4. Cool, filter using a 0.25 µm membrane, and purify by dialysis membrane for 24 h.

### Synthesis of CQD/ZnO Composites
1. Disperse 0.1 g ZnO in 10 mL deionized water.
2. Add solutions of CQDs (0.4 g, 0.8 g, 1.2 g, 2 g, 2.5 g NPCQD) to the dispersion.
3. Stir at room temperature for 12 h, then centrifuge and wash.
4. Dry at 80°C for 12 h to obtain the final composites.
